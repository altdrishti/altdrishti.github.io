Japi - Assam Holiday Calendar

The definitive holiday companion for Assam. We bridge the gap between official government schedules and traditional cultural timings. Featuring a unique dual-calendar system that keeps you connected to your roots while organizing your professional life.

  • Smart Holiday Filters: Distinguish instantly between Gazetted and Restricted holidays.
  • Cultural Integration: View Assamese dates (Tithi) alongside English dates.
  • Zero-Distraction Mode: Offline-first design with smart notifications that respect your sleep.
